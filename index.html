Who we are

Junior Chamber International (JCI) is a network of over a quarter million members all young leaders below 40 years, with their presence in more than 5000 communities of the world, located in 100 nations and territories. JCI has been in the forefront of creating platforms in harnessing opportunities.

JCI has an unparalleled ability to spread the principles of the United Nation’s Global Impact across the world and create positive change through advocating developmental opportunities that empower young people create a positive change because we believe that earth’s great treasures lies in human personality.

JCI Vision

JCI Creed

We believe,

  • That faith in God gives meaning and purpose to human life.
  • That the brotherhood of man transcends the sovereignty of nations.
  • That economic justice can be best won by freemen through free enterprise.
  • That government should be of law, rather than of man.
  • That earth's great treasure lies in human personality.
  • That service to humanity is the best work of life.

Message From The LOP

There is almost no limit to the potential of an organization that recruits good people, raises them up as leaders and continually develops them. -John C Maxwell. To enter The New Move you need a Paradigm Shift and my goal is to create a dynamic where the individual growth of every Jaycee creates a ripple effect of change in the organization and by extension the society.
Piggybacking off the part of the Creed which states that “Economic justice can best be won by free men through free enterprise” my plan for the 2021/2022 Local Organization year is to equip and transform every member with the necessary skills and spirit of excellence to be the “Queen” in the “Chess” game of life while forging long term sustainable relationships and partnerships. Growth requires sacrifice and being willing to do the work for a better tomorrow. Let us all be the leaders we wish we had. If I am to leave any parting words it would be “You are never going to be the best version of yourself unless you begin so just start, mess up, learn from it and move forward”.
